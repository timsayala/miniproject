ABOUT THIS COURSE

Getting started with the guitar? You need to take this course! We'll cover all the fundamentals and develop your
playing abilities so that you can play the guitar with assurance. By the end of the course, you'll be able to play a ton of excellent tunes, the eight fundamental open chords, and the most frequent strumming patterns!
